Savoury Stuffing Meatloaf

Description

This easy to make Meatloaf is the ultimate comfort food and is perfect for a midweek meal! Easy to make in just 55 minutes with just simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 1.5 pounds Ground Beef
  • 1 small Onion, chopped
  • 0.75 cup chicken-flavored bread stuffing mix (such as Kraft Stove Top)
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan with cooking spray.
  2. Mix beef, onion, stuffing mix, and egg by hand in a bowl. Press beef mixture into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 35 to 40 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  4. Top meatloaf with shredded mozzarella cheese and let rest while cheese melts, about 10 minutes.
